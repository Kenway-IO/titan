About TITAN

The methodology behind enterprise integration excellence

Our Mission

TITAN was developed by Kenway Consulting's IO Lab to address a critical gap in enterprise integration: the lack of a standardized, repeatable methodology for designing event-driven workflows that bridge modern SaaS applications, legacy ERP systems, and AI-powered decision engines.

The TITAN Philosophy

Event-Driven First: Business events should trigger immediate reactions, not batch processes.
Intelligence Embedded: AI/ML should be a first-class citizen in integration flows, not an afterthought.
Controlled Vocabulary: Standardized naming (T-I-T-A-N) creates common language across business and IT.
Value-Centric: Every workflow should quantify impact across capacity, speed, and quality dimensions.
