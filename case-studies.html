MANUFACTURING • $450k Annual Savings

Smart Rework Analysis with Computer Vision

Global manufacturer automates quality control decisions using AI-powered defect classification

The Challenge

A Fortune 500 automotive parts manufacturer was struggling with inconsistent quality control decisions during production. When defects were detected on the assembly line, human inspectors had to manually assess whether parts could be reworked or should be scrapped. This process was slow, subjective, and caused production delays.

With over 500 defect cases per day across 3 plants, the company faced:

  • 45-minute average decision time per defect (inspectors reviewing photos and specs)
  • Inconsistent decisions between inspectors (35% variance in rework vs. scrap calls)
  • Production line stops while waiting for QC approval
  • Over-scrapping due to conservative decision-making ($1.2M annual waste)

The TITAN Solution

We implemented an end-to-end TITAN workflow that combines SAP integration, Azure AI Vision, and automated decision-making:

T
TRIGGER
Defect logged in SAP S/4HANA by line operator
I
INGEST
Fetch defect images from Azure Blob Storage + part specifications
T
THINK
Azure AI Vision analyzes defect severity + ML model predicts rework feasibility
A
ACT
Update SAP with "REWORK" or "SCRAP" decision + route part to appropriate queue
N
NOTIFY
Alert supervisor via Teams + log decision rationale in Power BI dashboard

Tech Stack: SAP S/4HANA, Azure AI Vision, Azure Functions, Logic Apps, Power BI

Results

Reduction in QC Decision Time
From 45 min → 6 min average

Reduced scrap rate by 35%
99.2%
Decision Accuracy
vs. 65% human consistency
